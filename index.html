Model 3

Meet Model 3

  • Prioritizes ride quality and reliability while embracing a minimalist design, keeping the price low without sacrificing quality.
  • Charging is often cheaper than filling up a gas tank. Model 3 Standard is designed to make sure that each charge lasts as long as possible, maximizing your range.
  • Tesla vehicles have electric motors instead of gas engines. This reduces the amount of annual maintenance you will need to do to keep it running.
  • Premium and sporty, Model 3 Standard gets up to 321 miles of EPA estimated range with an efficient, aerodynamic body design and a smaller battery pack. An all-glass roof offers panoramic views from every seat.
  • A clean and precise interior provides everything you need inside your cabin.
  • Get first and second-row heated textile seats plus a heated steering wheel. Finished with premium materials throughout the cabin.

Meet Model Y

  • Model Y Performance can adapt to your driving style, speed and road conditions to keep your ride smooth and your pulse elevated.
  • Drive fast while staying in control. The design of the vehicle frame and the position of the wheels keep Model Y Performance responsive when steering through sharp turns and long curves.
  • Redesigned battery cells take you farther on a single charge. They hold more energy, helping to increase estimated range to 306 miles (EPA est.).
  • Your vehicle stays stable at high speeds and on bumpy roads thanks to a suspension that changes based on the road and how you drive it.
  • The materials used for the interior of Model Y Performance are handcrafted to make sure every surface and seat is comfortable.
  • A suite of advanced driver-assistance features designed to provide more active guidance and assisted driving under your active supervision.

Model S

Meet Model S

  • A 17.4” first-row and 9.4” second-row touchscreen offer a vivid image and respond quickly to your touch. Driver cluster display shows your speed, range and more.
  • Load up your bike, luggage and more. A hands-free liftgate provides flexible storage while second-row seats fold flat for more space.
  • Choose from infinite colors to customize your wrap-around lighting and enjoy dynamic animated illuminations that appear along the dash and door trims when you enter then fade to an ambient glow.
  • Enter your destination and your Model S will manage your drive, from start to finish. Full Self-Driving (Supervised) provides advanced features that offer more active guidance and assisted driving under your active supervision.
  • Three carbon-sleeved rotors and an enhanced battery system ensure maximum power without performance degradation, even during back-to-back runs.
  • A high-performance powertrain and advanced battery system unlock instant torque, efficiency and range. An optimized thermal system allows for faster charging and more consistent power at the track or on the road.

Meet Model X

  • Supportive, ventilated front-row seats and heated seats throughout the cabin provide added comfort for every passenger. Integrated air vents, tri-zone climate controls and HEPA filtration create your ideal cabin atmosphere.
  • Dual motor all-wheel drive unlocks the longest range in our line-up and is built for powerful performance in all conditions and long-distance road trips.
  • With a wider stance and refined aerodynamics, you can handle corners with more precision and feel more planted at high speeds.
  • Staggered wheels and tires maximize grip and stability while carbon ceramic brakes5 on Plaid provide extra stopping power.
  • Drive up to 410 miles on a single charge. When you need to recharge, add up to 205 miles in just 15 minutes at one of over 75,000 Superchargers globally.
  • Safety at Tesla is paramount. Every Tesla vehicle is designed with impact-absorbing zones, a stiff passenger compartment to minimize intrusion and multiple airbags to help protect occupants.
